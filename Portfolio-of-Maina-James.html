About Me

Hello, I'm Maina James

With over 8 years of experience in graphic design, I've had the privilege of working with businesses across Kenya and beyond to create compelling visual identities that resonate with their audiences.

Based in the beautiful town of Nyahururu, I combine my formal training in graphic design with a passion for African art and contemporary design trends to create unique visual solutions that tell your brand's story.
